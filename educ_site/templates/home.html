about us

Research, Digital Texts, and Reading Comprehension

We are a Group of Researchers Conducting a Study About Teacher's Perception on the Effectiveness of Digital Texts' in Promoting the Reading Comprehension of Elementary Learners

his Website Serves as Compilation and Source of Reading Materials, Reading Activities, and Video Presentations that can be Accessed, Viewed, and Downloaded by the Teachers which they can Use in Utilizing Digital Texts in Teaching the Elementary Learners

our Materials

Video Presentations

Every Video Presentation Contains Engaging and Meaningful Content and Information that will Capture the Interest of the Learners in Reading Digital Texts that can be Seen in the Video Presentations which is Intended for the Enhancement of their Reading Comprehension.

Lessons

Every Reading Material can be a Step Towards Developing Greater Reading Skills and Understanding of the Learners while Every Activities which is about the Reading Materials can be an Effective Way to Enhance and Evaluate the Learners' Reading Knowledge and Comprehension.
